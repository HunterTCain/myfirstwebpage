My Favorite Places

Here I'll talk about some of my favorite places!

Downtown Boise

Downtown Boise is an awesome place because this is where a lot of my life happens. Some of my favorite things about this place is:

  • The STIL

    • This is where I work and scoop ice cream.

    Here is their Website

  • PROST!

    • This is the best spot in Boise to grab a beer. It,s right in the heart of 8th Street and has the best brews around. My friends and I meet here every Wednesday night.

    Here is a link to their website

  • Freak Alley

    • The famous freak ally in Boise features different art installations from local artists! Here are some of my favorites:

    • Mirror Portraits
    • Space Squirrel
    • Mother Earth

Frontier Ranch

Frontier is a very special place and where I worked during the summer.

My job here was ropes which was very fun and had some interesting duties. Here are some of them:

  • Training summer staff in the ropes elements
  • Inspecting ropes elements and submitting instection forms
  • Helping set up different events and running them
  • Motivating and facilitating rappels and swings
